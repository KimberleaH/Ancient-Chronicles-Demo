Okiri

o-keer-ee

Planet: Soros

Race: Fezsa

Age: 32 YS

Height: 4' 6" / 137 cm

Core Type: Light Light

Occupation: Keeper of Soma

Pronouns: she/her

Former Tutor and Guard of the High Seren Royal Family

The majority of Okiri's life was spent in servitude. She started as a servant of the High Seren community, living on the impoverished midland shelf working the land and growing crops for the upper lands' residents. As a young adult, she was fancied as a house worker, providing for a noble seren family for many years. The family's status and connections with the royal family — as well as Okiri's prowess with light energy — eventually led to Okiri serving as a royal guard and an official medic for any who needed it. However, her duties expanded when the royal family bore a son. The crown prince. With his birth, Okiri made it her mission to teach him — in secret — lessons that would make him question the actions of his people and all those before him.

When she and her partner inadvertently had kits of their own, she wanted nothing more than for them to live life freely. Out from under the tyrannous boot of the High Seren. She lost her mate in the operation that would get them and several others off the planet. Serpentis became her new home where she met Soma and was invited to take up the role of Keeper.
